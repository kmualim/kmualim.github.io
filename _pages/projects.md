      <h2>Community &amp; Outreach Projects</h2>

      <ul class="project-list">

        <li class="project-item">
          <div class="project-header">
            <span class="project-title">Wonderfest Science Envoy</span>
            <span class="project-role">2025 Science Envoy · Wonderfest Bay Area</span>
          </div>
          <p>
            Selected as a 2025 Wonderfest Science Envoy, a program recognizing science communicators
            dedicated to making research accessible to Bay Area communities. Kristy's envoy work spans
            her research on plant climate adaptation, global genetic diversity loss, and community-led
            initiatives building climate resilience.
          </p>
          <div class="project-links">
            <a href="https://wonderfest.org/kristy-mualim/" target="_blank">Wonderfest profile</a>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="project-item">
          <div class="project-header">
            <span class="project-title">Bay Area Urban Heat Island Mapping Campaign</span>
            <span class="project-role">Lead Organizer</span>
          </div>
          <p>
            A community science initiative under NOAA's Urban Heat Island Mapping program measuring
            temperature and humidity across Palo Alto, East Palo Alto, and Menlo Park.
            Volunteers drove pre-planned routes with vehicle-mounted sensors on a hot summer day to
            produce fine-grained heat maps that inform urban planning, housing, and climate equity policy.
            The campaign was covered by <a href="https://www.ktvu.com/video/1506740" target="_blank">KTVU FOX 2</a>
            and is supported by the Schmidt Family Foundation, NOAA, Acterra, and CAPA Strategies.
          </p>
          <div class="project-links">
            <a href="https://kmualim.github.io/bayareaheatmapping2024/about/" target="_blank">Project site</a>
            <a href="https://www.ktvu.com/video/1506740" target="_blank">KTVU coverage</a>
            <a href="https://www.paloalto.gov/Events-Directory/Public-Works/August-Bay-Area-Urban-Heat-Island-Mapping-Campaign" target="_blank">City of Palo Alto</a>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="project-item">
          <div class="project-header">
            <span class="project-title">The Land Around Us</span>
            <span class="project-role">Co-creator &amp; Researcher</span>
          </div>
          <p>
            A short documentary following ecology graduate students as they travel across the Greater
            San Francisco Bay Area to interview four experts in land stewardship — exploring how
            individuals can better connect with and protect local land. The film was funded by the
            <a href="https://ethicsinsociety.stanford.edu/node/14026/past-collaborative-research-projects" target="_blank">McCoy Center for Ethics in Society</a>
            at Stanford through their Collaborative Research Grant, and is available to screen
            in communities throughout the Bay Area.
          </p>
          <div class="project-links">
            <a href="https://vickyzchan.com/#thelandaroundus" target="_blank">Watch the film</a>
            <a href="https://ethicsinsociety.stanford.edu/node/14026/past-collaborative-research-projects" target="_blank">Ethics grant</a>
          </div>
        </li>

        <li class="project-item">
          <div class="project-header">
            <span class="project-title">Ethics in Society Fellow</span>
            <span class="project-role">Fellow · McCoy Center, Stanford</span>
          </div>
          <p>
